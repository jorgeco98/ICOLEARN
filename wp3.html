ICoLearn - Internet and collaborative based learning methodologies toolbox for digital transformation and sustainable growth | Project No.: 101127224

Work Package 3

Design and Organization

Duration:M13 – M24
Lead Beneficiary:6- UCR
Objectives:

  • Define the detailed procedures and programs for implementation of learning methodologies internet-based and collaborative tools in the selected subjects (curricula units) at the Partner Countries HEI, for each curricula unit involved.
  • Selection of enterprises which will provide tasks/assignments for students for LF and BL.
  • Detailed specification of tasks/assignments for implementation of learning methodologies internet-based and collaborative tools in the selected subjects (curricula units), at the Partner Countries partners’ universities for application within the each subject (curricula unit).
  • Scheduling plan for the teaching and students work on pre-defined tasks/assignments along the semester, for implementation, and realization, of the learning methodologies internet-based and collaborative tools for each of the selected subjects (curricula units), at the Partner Countries partners’ universities.

    • Deliverables

  • D3.1 - Tool application design and procedure
  • D3.2 - “Brokering Event”
  • D3.3 - Assignments and Scheduling plan
