UNIVERSIDAD NACIONAL AUTÓNOMA DE HONDURAS

https://www.unah.edu.hn/
  • Founded in 1847
  • It is the largest public university of Honduras
  • It has approximately 65,000 students, over 4,000 academics and 2,500 administratives
  • The main campus (red circle) is in Tegucigalpa (the capital of Honduras) and there are 9
    regional campuses
  • There are 5 virtual centres (telecentros) and an additional 8 long distance centres (craed)


Faculties

  • There are 10 faculties in the main campus:
  • Social Sciences
  • Pharmacy and Chemistry
  • Dentistry
  • Legal Sciences
  • Engineering
  • Humanities and Arts
  • Space Sciences
  • Economic and Administrative Sciences
  • Sciences
  • Medical Sciences



Other Services

  • UNAH Also has:
  • 8 observatories
  • 10 research institutes, including the Applied
    Sciences and Technologies, which is managed
    by the Engineering Faculty
  • 4 specialized centres, including the Extended
    Reality Innovation Centre, which is managed
    by the Engineering Faculty
